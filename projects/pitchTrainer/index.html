What note do you think it is?

Press the "Pick random note" button to choose a new note, and then press play. Try to guess what the note is by choosing it from the drop-down list, and then submitting your guess by pressing "Guess!". If your guessed note comes up red, you guessed wrong. Don't worry! You can try again until you get it right, when the note will come up green!
